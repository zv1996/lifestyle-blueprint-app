Frequently Asked Questions

How does the meal planning chatbot work?

Our meal planning chatbot uses advanced AI to understand your dietary preferences, restrictions, and goals. It then creates personalized meal plans tailored specifically to your needs, taking into account factors like calorie requirements, macronutrient balance, and food preferences.

Can I customize my meal plans?

Absolutely! You can customize any meal plan by telling the chatbot your preferences. If you don't like a particular meal, you can ask for alternatives. You can also adjust portion sizes, swap ingredients, or request completely different meals.

Is there a subscription fee?

We offer both free and premium subscription options. The free plan allows you to create basic meal plans with limited customization. Our premium subscription provides unlimited meal plans, advanced customization options, nutritional analysis, and grocery lists.

Can I access my meal plans offline?

Yes! As a Progressive Web App (PWA), Lifestyle Blueprint can be installed on your device and accessed offline. Your previously created meal plans will be available even without an internet connection.
